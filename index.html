Anaïs Jeanine Ramón

I empathize, then create.

Exploring the intersections of art, design, and technology.

Sak pasé! Soy Anaïs (Ah-nah-ees). I’ve been designing tings in NYC since 2013. RIT alum, with a degree in Graphic Design and a minor in Spanish Language and Culture. I’m interested in creating more opportunities for Black, Indigenous, and/or Latin American people in design and tech.

Currently getting shit done as a Senior UX/UI Designer at Tivix.

Beyond blessed to call El Barrio Artspace home.
